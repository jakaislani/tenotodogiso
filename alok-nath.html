Alok Nath is considered as the most adored and most famous on-screen father. He was born in Khagaria, Bihar, India on July 10, 1956. Alok Nath has portrayed humble, down-to-earth and true father roles in a number of Bollywood movies. This highly dedicated and strong-minded actor has amused the viewers from his Babuji role accompanied by many other roles.

Alok Nath has a prolonged list of films to credit though he is mainly recognized for his successful movies such as Hum Aapke Hain Koun, Maine Pyar Kiya, Vivah, Hum Saath-Saath Hain, Taal and Pardes. In the year 1980, he initiated his career with TV serial titled Rishte-Naate. Though in 1987, he got credit with his role of Haveli Ram in television sitcom Buniyaad. In 1982, Alok Nath made his first Bollywood debut with the movie titled Gandhi. This highly talented actor has always endeavored hard to validate his flexibility of work. Alok Nath has also portrayed negative roles in movies such as Vinashak, Shadyantra and Bol Radha Bol. He has starred in more than 500 Bollywood movies and 40 to 50 television shows. He has appeared in numerous regional movies as well.

In recent times, Alok Nath has starred in a television serial titled Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai aired on Star Plus. In last couple of years, memes and jokes specifically based upon his role became viral across social media. He accepted those jokes cheerfully and liked them. Alok is a father which every other television show or movies desires to portray.

Alok Nath belongs to a Brahmin Hindu family. He has a sister named Vineeta Malik who is also an actress by profession. He married to famous production subordinate Ashu Singh. The couple was blessed with a son named Shivang Nath and a daughter named Junhai Nath.
