Ruby Soho AEW Age Wikipedia: How Old Is The Wrestler?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Ruby Soho is now officially an AEW wrestler and is still acing her wrestling moves at the age of 30. Get to know her more closer below!

Ruby Soho is an American professional wrestler.

She is predominantly known as ‘Ruby Riott’ in WWE where she fought alongside her two partners; Liv Morgan and Sarah Logan.

As of September 5, she took the wrestling fandom by surprise when she debuted in AEW’s pay-per-view event ‘All Out”.

Her new character in the company and her name change have been widely well-received by the viewers.

Ruby Soho Age And Wikipedia Explored

Ruby Soho made her AEW debut at the age of 30 on September 5. She was born in 1991 and celebrates her birthday on January 9 every year.

She started training with Billy Roc in professional wrestling in 2010. Her first name in the ring was Heidi Lovelace to which she has openly shared her disliking.

She officially hit the ring with more experiences through her stints with Ohio Valley Wrestling, Shine Wrestling, Chikara, World Wonder RingStardom from 2012 to 2015.

Ruby started her WWE career from its sister company NXT with the name Ruby Riot in 2016. She was then promoted to the main roster in 2017 with two new partners.

Ruby led the group called “The Riot Squad” and modified her name to Ruby Riott. She underwent shoulder surgery to repair a bilateral injury and again underwent the same procedure on her other shoulder in August 2019.

She was effectively working in the WWE Smackdown roster until she was released by the company in June this year.

It was after that she debuted on AEW main event and won the women’s Casino Battle Royale match.

Thus, she has earned a future match for the AEW Women’s World Championship, a very good start in her career.

What Is Her Real Name?

Ruby Soho’s real name is Dori Prange though she is widely known by her stage name.

Throughout her career, she has performed with many names; among which Ruby Riott is the most famous one.

She was keen on taking the same name to the AEW ring but WWE owns the copyright to the name which cannot be overturned.

Hence, with the help of Rancid band’s member Lars Fredriksen, she took on the name “Ruby Soho” from the song of the same name.

The official name change was done via her Instagram handle. Her Instagram handle is mostly dedicated to her career updates.

Ruby Soho Net Worth Revealed

Ruby Soho surely maintains a notable amount of net worth figures from her decade-long wrestling career.

However, she has not opened up about her earning figures and her personal assets as of today.
