Kim Zolciak Age: How Old Is Kim Zolciak?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Kimberleigh Marie Zolciak-Biermann is an American TV personality and singer, best known for her appearances on The Real Housewives of Atlanta. Born on May 19, 1978, in Pensacola, Florida, Zolciak-Biermann grew up in Windsor Locks, Connecticut, in a military family. She is of Polish and Italian descent and was raised Roman Catholic. She graduated from East Catholic High School in Manchester, Connecticut in 1996, and studied nursing at the University of Connecticut.

In 2008, Zolciak-Biermann appeared as one of the original housewives on the reality television series The Real Housewives of Atlanta. She left the show during its fifth season but returned as a “friend of the housewives” for the tenth season in 2017-18. She also had her own spin-off show titled Don’t Be Tardy for the Wedding, which focused on the preparation of her wedding to football player Kroy Biermann. The series was later renamed Don’t Be Tardy and aired for eight seasons until the final episode on December 29, 2020.

Apart from her television appearances, Zolciak-Biermann was also into music. In 2008, she began work on a country music album and released her debut single, “Tardy for the Party,” in 2009. She also competed on the 21st season of Dancing with the Stars in 2015 but withdrew three weeks into the competition due to a transient ischemic attack. Zolciak-Biermann was also scheduled to star in a reality show with fellow The Real Housewives cast member, NeNe Leakes, but the network later decided not to move forward with the show.

In March 2013, Zolciak-Biermann was sued by her Real Housewives of Atlanta castmate Kandi Burruss and her collaborating songwriter/producer Rodney “Don Vito” Richard for profits earned from “Tardy for the Party.”

What Is Kim Zolciak Age?

As of 2023, Kim Zolciak is 44 years old. Her birthplace is Pensacola, Florida and she was born on May 19, 1978 to parents Joseph and Karen.
