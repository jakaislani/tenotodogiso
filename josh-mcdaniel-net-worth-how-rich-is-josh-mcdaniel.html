How Rich Is Josh McDaniel?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 12, 2024

Josh McDaniels is an American professional football coach who earns a salary of $6 million. His net worth is $6 million.

In April 1976, Josh McDaniels was born in Barberton, Ohio. He played wide receiver at John Carroll University while attending Canton McKinley High School.

From 1999 to 2000, McDaniels worked as a graduate assistant at Michigan State University, where he began his coaching career.

He started working as a personnel assistant for the NFL’s New England Patriots in 2001.

From 2002 to 2003, McDaniels worked as the Patriots’ defensive coaching assistant before taking over as the team’s quarterbacks coach from 2004 to 2008.

He also worked for New England as its offensive coordinator from 2006 to 2008.

From 2009 to 2010, McDaniels served as the head coach of the Denver Broncos. He served as the St. Louis Rams’ offensive coordinator in 2011 before joining the Patriots once more as an offensive assistant.

McDaniels has been the New England Patriots’ offensive coordinator since 2012. He was the NFL’s youngest head coach at the time of his hire, aged 33. In 2007, McDaniels became embroiled in a videotaping controversy, for which the NFL penalized him $50,000.
