Awra Briguela Scandal And Controversy

Awra Briguela finds herself during a scandal and controversy that unfolded on June 29.

A social media video showed the 19-year-old actress being detained by police in front of a pub in Makati City.

According to the Southern Police District (SPD), Awra allegedly engaged in a confrontation with a group of men, demanding that one of them remove his shirt.

She reportedly tore his shirt when her request was denied, resulting in a physical altercation.

Awra received support from netizens and prominent celebrities. Notably, YouTuber Riva Quenery, a longtime friend of Awra, vocally defended her.

Riva raised questions about why Awra was the only one apprehended and arrested, demanding a more thorough investigation into the involvement of other men in the altercation.

In the aftermath of the incident, Zayla Nakajima, a content creator also with Briguela at the bar, utilized social media to dispel the circulating rumours.

Why Was Filipino Comedian Awra Briguela Arrested?

Briguela, a well-known Filipino comedian, was arrested after being involved in a brawl outside a pub in Makati City.

The incident led to criminal charges against her, including physical injuries, alarm and scandal, disobedience to authority, and direct assault.

The Southern Police District (SPD) reported that Awra allegedly caused a disturbance at the Bolt Hole Bar and was apprehended by the police around 5 am on Thursday.

Complainant Mark Christian Ravana said the altercation occurred during a farewell party.

Awra and several others approached Ravana’s group and allegedly initiated a confrontation. The situation escalated when Awra violently tore Ravana’s shirt, and her friends joined in.

When the police intervened, Awra reportedly became verbally abusive towards the officers, disregarding their authority. She also pushed one of the arresting officers, leading to her arrest.
