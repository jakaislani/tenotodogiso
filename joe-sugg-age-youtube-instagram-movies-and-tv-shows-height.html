Joseph Graham Sugg is an English YouTuber and actor.

Joe Sugg Age

Hoe old is Joe Sugg? Joe Sugg is 32 years old. He was born on 8 September 1991, in Lacock, Wiltshire, England

Joe Sugg YouTube

Does Joe Sugg have a YouTube channel? Joe Sugg has a YouTube channel ThatcherJoe, currently at over 7 million subscribers.

Joe Sugg Instagram

Is on Instagram? Joe Sugg is on Instagram at https://www.instagram.com/joe_sugg/

Joe Sugg Movies and tv shows

What movies and tv show shave Joe Sugg been in? Joe Sugg voiced Gus in the UK version of the film Wonder Park (2019). In May 2019, he guest starred on the Cartoon Network show, The Amazing World of Gumball, as the voice of Azreal.

In July 2019, Sugg announced that he would have a cameo voiceover role in the 2019 Aardman film A Shaun the Sheep Movie: Farmageddon. He portrayed Ogie Anhorn in the West End production of Waitress.  In 2018, he was a finalist on the sixteenth series of Strictly Come Dancing

Joe Sugg Height

How tall is Joe Sugg? Joe Sugg is 5′ 8½″ (1.74 m) tall.
