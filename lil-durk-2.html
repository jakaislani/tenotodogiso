Lil Durk aka Durk Derrick Banks is an American Singer, Songwriter and Rapper. As of 2023, Lil Durk’s net worth is $3 million. He acclaimed recognition as the lead member and founder of the Chicago rap collection, titled “Only the Family”.

He has collaborated with numerous popular artists such as A Boogie with da Hoodie, Future, Kevin Gates, and Ty Dolla Sign during his career. He stays connected with his fans through his photos and posts on social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and others.

Lil Durk Wiki/Biography

Born on 19 October 1992, Lil Durk’s age is 30 Years Old as of 2023. He took birth and was raised in a poverty-stricken family from Englewood, Chicago, Illinois, United States. He is known to be an American by nationality and he has belief in Muslim religion.

Lil Durk Childhood Pic

Lil Durk attended Paul Robeson High School but dropped out to join a street rappers gang in Chicago called “Black Disciples”. He was a passionate lover of music since his childhood, but due to his very humble background, he never got a chance to take music lessons.

Career

Lil Durk embarked on the journey towards his career by uploading videos on MySpace and YouTube like other renowned rappers. He is the owner of a record label collective titled “Only The Family” and has collaborated with many popular musicians such as Travis Scott, Young Thug, Rowdy Rebel, etc. He has launched numerous albums, singles, and mixtapes.

He debuted with the album titled “Remember My Name” in 2015 with Only The Family”.He launched his other hit albums “Lil Durk 2X” in 2016 and “Signed to the Streets 3” in 2018. “Love Songs 4 the Streets 2”, “Just Cause Y’all Waited for 2”, “The Voice” are his other successful albums. Moreover, he has launched an album titled “The Voice of the Heroes” with Lil Baby in 2021.
