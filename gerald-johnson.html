Career Overview

Johnson started his career as a hip-hop artist and recorded various songs. After that, he began his on-screen career as Murder Mike in the 2007 movie Gangsta Rap: The Glockumentary.

Following it, he appeared as a side character in many TV series and movies. His famous films include Community Service, Six Guys One Car, The Christian Show, and The Outdoorsman.

Currently, he is working with the crew of the upcoming five movies and TV series at once. His forthcoming projects are I Left My Heart in Colombia, White Men Can't Jump, Work, Fire, Kings of L.A and We Got This.
