Ex WWE Star Lana Debuts At AEW All Out

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 29, 2024

AEW held All Out in Chicago, and fans were treated to quite a surprise after Miro’s match with Powerhouse Hobbs. Lana, Miro’s real-life wife, who has been going by CJ Perry since her WWE release, finally joined her husband in All Elite Wrestling.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW All Out.

Lana was a star of Total Divas during her time with WWE. A lot of fans wondered if she would join Miro in AEW, and while he seemingly teased her coming in, that hasn’t happened yet. Everything changed at All Out.

Miro beat Powerhouse Hobbs at All Out, but then he got beatdown after the match. Then CJ Perry showed up, which was a big surprise.

Miro looked shocked, and he didn’t know how to act to CJ Perry showing up in AEW. He left, leaving a cliffhanger in the process. Now, we will have to see how this storyline pans out on AEW television.

AEW has featured Miro on Collision since his return from a long hiatus. Now, it appears that he has a very intriguing angle on his hands!

Both Miro and CJ Perry were released from WWE during the pandemic. Although Miro found a home in AEW, CJ Perry focused on other endeavors. Now, she has finally joined her husband in Tony Khan’s company. This was a long time coming for a lot of fans, as it also placed WWE’s old Lana and Rusev pair back together on television.
